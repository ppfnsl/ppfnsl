Planting Peace Forum Network

Planting Peace Forum Network (PPFN) is a youth and student-led peacebuilding organization dedicated to promoting peace, purpose, and positive transformation in schools and communities. It provides a platform for young people to become agents of change—addressing issues such as drug abuse, conflict, violence, and social neglect, while empowering vulnerable children and youth to grow into responsible, peace-conscious citizens.

Our Core Services

Goal

Promote peace, purpose, and positive transformation among young people, especially in schools and communities, by addressing critical issues such as drug abuse, violence, conflict, and social neglect.

VISION

Our vision is to build a peaceful and purpose driven generation by planting the seed of peace, unity and responsibilities in the heart of young people children and communities across Sierra Leone and beyond.

MISSION

Our mission is to promote peace through education, outreach, advocacy and empowerment. The organization provides safe space for vulnerable children and youths, support school outreach program and inspire young people to become ambassadors of peace and transformation
